The Real Talk

The Demo Looks Easy.
Production Is Different.

Controlled environments make AI look magical. But deploying agents inside a real company comes with challenges you cannot ignore.

🔒

Data Security & Access Control

What data does the agent access? How do you prevent sensitive company data from leaking through an LLM call?

📋

Compliance & Governance

PDPA, financial regulations, audit trails. Autonomous agents need oversight frameworks — not just working code.

🔗

System Integration

Your agent needs to connect to your CRM, ERP, internal APIs. That's where most AI projects silently fail.

⚠️

Hallucination in Production

An agent giving wrong answers in a demo is embarrassing. In production, it costs clients, money, and trust.
